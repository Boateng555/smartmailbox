Step 1: Turn On Your Device

Simply turn on your Smart Camera using the power button or switch. The LED indicator will start blinking to show it's working.

What to look for:

  • Press the power button or flip the switch to turn on
  • LED light will start blinking (this means it's working)
  • Device is ready when LED blinks steadily
